## Overview

**Print Evolution** is a local company offering printing and design services for both private customers and other businesses.

For this project, I worked on a new high-fidelity web design concept intended to present the company in a more premium and contemporary way. The focus was visual communication: turning the character of the business into a clearer website concept across multiple screen sizes.

## Design Direction

The design aimed to balance two needs:

- communicate quality and professionalism for business clients
- remain approachable for private customers looking for custom print services

To do that, I worked on a visual language that felt clean, product-focused, and suitable for a service business with a broad range of services. I wanted it to feel more premium without slipping into the kind of sterile corporate aesthetic that makes every company look interchangeable.

The layouts were developed in high fidelity for both desktop and mobile, with attention to hierarchy, clarity, and presentation. Since the project was design-led, most of the value was in defining the tone of the website before any implementation work would begin.

## Outcome

This project is best understood as a design study rather than a full website build. Even in that scope, it was a useful exercise in shaping a stronger visual identity for a local business and presenting service-based content in a more structured and credible way.

Not every interesting web project starts with code. Sometimes the real work is deciding how a business should look and speak online before anyone opens an editor.
