# Cookie Policy

Last updated: 05/05/2026

---

## 1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files that websites save on the user's device while browsing. They may be used to ensure the proper functioning of the website, remember the user's preferences, or enable specific third-party services.

---

## 2. Cookies and tools used by this website

This website uses:

- a necessary first-party technical cookie to store the user's privacy/cookie preferences
- Google Analytics 4, only after the user's consent to analytics cookies and similar technologies
- Cloudflare Turnstile, only after the user's consent to third-party security verification for the contact form

The website does not load optional analytics tools before the user has made a choice through the cookie banner.

This website does not use marketing cookies, advertising pixels, newsletter tracking cookies, quote calculator cookies, or profiling tools.

---

## 3. Necessary technical cookies

Necessary technical cookies do not require the user's consent.

This website currently uses the following first-party technical cookie:

| Name | Provider | Type | Purpose | Indicative duration | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `dc_consent` | Davide Cuni | First-party technical cookie | Stores the user's privacy/cookie preferences and the policy version associated with the user's choice | Up to 12 months | Necessary to remember the preferences expressed through the cookie banner |

## 4. Optional categories subject to consent

The website provides optional categories that are activated only after the user's explicit consent.

### 4.1 Analytics

If enabled by the website owner and accepted by the user, the website may use:

- **Google Analytics 4**

This category is used to measure website traffic and performance in aggregate form.

### 4.2 Third-party security verification

The website may use:

- **Cloudflare Turnstile**, for anti-spam verification on the contact form

Cloudflare Turnstile may process technical data and use tokens, cookies, or similar identifiers according to Cloudflare's own documentation, browser behavior, and service configuration.

If consent is not provided, Cloudflare Turnstile is not loaded and the contact form cannot be submitted through the website. The user may still contact the Data Controller through the published email address.

## 6. Consent management

On the first visit, the cookie banner allows the user to:

- accept optional categories
- reject optional categories
- customize the choice by category

The user may change their preferences at any time through the **Manage Cookies** control available on the website.

If consent is not provided, optional analytics services and optional third-party security verification are not loaded.

---

## 7. How to manage or disable cookies

The user may manage cookie and third-party service preferences:

- through the preference management panel available on the website
- through the settings of their browser

Disabling necessary technical cookies may affect the proper functioning of some website features.

---

## 8. Further information

Technical server logs used for security and website operation are not cookies and are described in the website's Privacy Policy.

For more information on the processing of personal data, please refer to the **Privacy Policy**.
